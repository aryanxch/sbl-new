SBL ENERGYNEO™ OD
OVERVIEW

Ordinary Detonator — for safe initiation with safety fuse.

SBL Energy manufactures ordinary detonators under the brand name NEO™ OD (PESO ID 1106). This plain aluminium detonator is non-electric and is commonly used with safety fuse.

It is a No. 8 strength detonator, 37 mm in length with a shell diameter of 3.7 mm, produced in a modern plant using advanced manufacturing techniques.

Its No. 8 strength ensures effective initiation of cap-sensitive explosives, and it is fired when a length of safety fuse is inserted into the cap.
