SBL ENERGYNEO™ GEL - 90 CPT
OVERVIEW

Seismic explosive engineered to sink in water.

SBL Energy manufactures NEO™ GEL-90 CPT, a Seismic Emulsion Explosive designed specifically for seismic exploration.

Its density is engineered so that the cartridge sinks in water, making it ideal for sub-surface and water-filled borehole seismic surveys.

Supplied in a couplable tube format, it delivers high strength, high VOD and excellent water resistance, meeting the demanding requirements of oil & gas seismic exploration.

KEY ADVANTAGES

Why our Seismic Explosives stand out.

  • ✓High strength explosive — consistent energy delivery
  • ✓High Velocity of Detonation (5,000 m/s) — strong shock signature
  • ✓Excellent water resistance — hydrostatic head of 58 metres
  • ✓Packed in couplable tubes for easy field deployment
  • ✓Density designed so cartridge sinks in water — suitable for water-filled holes
