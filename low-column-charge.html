OVERVIEW

OPTIGEL — engineered column charging.

OPTIGEL is a low-density, non-cap-sensitive Class 2 emulsion explosive, manufactured by mixing an emulsion matrix with ammonium nitrate prills and HSD (high-speed diesel).

Application: OPTIGEL cartridges are suitable for large-diameter holes of 100 mm or more, where boreholes are dry or only damp with little water present. As a non-cap-sensitive explosive, it is used as a column charge in open-cut mining, quarrying and general blasting. For charging, the cartridge is simply dumped into the borehole, as with similar cartridge products.

KEY ADVANTAGES

Why our Low Column Charge Explosives stand out.

  • ✓Reliable and easy to use, provides consistent result
  • ✓Low density product — hence high column height with minimum explosive charge
  • ✓Cost effective explosive for dry hole or damp holes
  • ✓Higher heave energy compared to Slurry / Emulsion Cartridge Explosives — results in better fragmentation with less vibration
  • ✓Heave energy equivalent to ANFO

How OPTIGEL is used

A breakdown of application, initiation and packaging for the OPTIGEL low column charge explosive.

OPTIGEL Non-Cap-Sensitive Class II · Column Charge

OPTIGEL

APPLICATION
  • Deep / long-hole blasting in opencast mines, quarrying and hill cutting
  • Used as Column Charge in dry holes

HOW TO USE
  • Non-cap-sensitive / non-aluminized explosive — must be used with a Cap-Sensitive explosive / Booster Charge
