NEO™ E-DET

NEO™ E-DET (Neo-edet) is an electronic detonator system designed for precise and reliable blast initiation in mining, quarrying and construction. It enables accurate control of delay timing, helping achieve consistent fragmentation, improved blast control and efficient use of explosives.

The system operates on a digital two-wire communication network that allows straightforward field connection, blast programming and verification before firing. Integrated protection and safety controls — spark-gap protection, thermal-barrier encapsulation and hardware-plus-software interlocks — help prevent unintended initiation and ensure stable operation during handling and firing.

up to 500 det / blast Initiation Capacity
25,000 ms Max Programmable Delay
0.1% ± 0.5 ms Timing Accuracy
−20 to 80 °C Operating Range

Used with the NEO-EDET™ Logger Cum Blaster. Full electrical, wire and shell specifications are in the datasheet.
