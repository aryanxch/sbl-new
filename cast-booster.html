OVERVIEW

High-strength PETN + TNT cast boosters.

Cast boosters are designed to deliver high initiation and detonation pressure to a wide range of explosives and blasting agents. They consist of high-density molecular explosives — PETN and TNT — which are sensitive to intense impact, heat or friction, housed in a plastic shell.

Each booster has two longitudinal passages that accommodate a detonator or detonating cord. A cast booster is used when a detonator alone does not have enough energy to initiate an explosive directly: the detonator fires the booster, which in turn delivers the shock wave to the explosive or blasting agent. A recessed well at the base protects the detonating cord, signal tube or lead wires from damage.

Cast boosters offer a long storage and shelf life, high resistance to water and oil, and easy operation, making them convenient to handle and assemble.

SBL Energy offers cast boosters in 25 g, 100 g, 250 g and 500 g sizes to suit different requirements.
