OVERVIEW

On-site, site-mixed bulk emulsion systems.

Bulk, or site-mixed, explosive systems are designed for customers who require large quantities of explosives. The materials are usually mixed on site by the supplier in the delivery truck and drawn directly into the drill hole by trained workers. This approach is intended for quarry and open-pit mining, where it improves blast execution, safety and environmental performance.

The material is transported as an oxidiser and sensitised using emulsion processing technology during borehole loading. At SBL Energy, the bulk system is available for emulsion explosives only.

SAFETY GUIDELINES

Handling, storage
& transport.

Always follow local PESO regulations and SBL Energy's product-specific safety guidance.

  • !Should be kept away from heat / sparks / open flames / hot surfaces
  • !Eliminate all potential sources of ignition — avoid activities that could create an impact, friction, spark or a sudden rise in temperature
