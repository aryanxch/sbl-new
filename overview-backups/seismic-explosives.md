## Heading
Seismic explosive engineered to sink in water.

## Paragraphs (3)

### Paragraph 1 (15 words)
SBL Energy manufactures NEO GEL-90 CPT, a Seismic Emulsion Explosive designed specifically for seismic exploration.

### Paragraph 2 (22 words)
The product density is engineered so that the cartridge sinks in water, making it ideal for sub-surface and water-filled borehole seismic surveys.

### Paragraph 3 (26 words)
Packed in couplable tube format, it delivers high strength, high VOD and excellent water resistance — meeting the demanding requirements of oil & gas seismic exploration.
