# copper-delay-detonator — Overview (ORIGINAL BACKUP)

## Heading
Reliable delay initiation for underground coal mines.

## Paragraphs (2)

### Paragraph 1 (37 words)
This detonator is made of copper shells filled with PETN as the base charge mixed with a primary charge. The shells are crimped with a pair of PVC-coated wires and are fitted to an electric fuse head.

### Paragraph 2 (30 words)
Available as NEO CDD (PESO 634) — Permitted Copper Delay Detonator — and NEO CED (PESO 633) — Permitted Copper Instantaneous Detonator. Both are PESO-approved for use in coal mines.
