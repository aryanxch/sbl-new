# detonating-fuse — Overview (ORIGINAL BACKUP)

## Heading
PETN at the core — safe & quick non-electric initiation.

## Paragraphs (3)

### Paragraph 1 (41 words)
Detonating cord or fuse is a core of high explosives, normally PETN (pentaerythritol tetranitrate), enclosed in PP yarn, plastic sheathing (polyethene jacket) & natural & synthetic fibre for waterproofing. It is basically PETN in covering of textile, plastic & waterproofing material.

### Paragraph 2 (36 words)
The degree of tensile strength, abrasion resistance, & flexibility of each covering varies. It acts as a means for safe & quick initiation of detonator-sensitive industrial explosives & provides a path for initiation of non-electric detonators.

### Paragraph 3 (36 words)
Available in four variants — NEO CORD (10 g/m), NEO CORD 8 (8 g/m), NEO CORD 12 (12 g/m) and NEO CORD 20 (20 g/m) — with PESO Brand IDs 1031, 1153, 1154 and 1155 respectively.
