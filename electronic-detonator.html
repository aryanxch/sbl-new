OVERVIEW

Programmable electronic detonator system.

NEO E-DET is a fit-for-purpose, electronic detonator–based blasting system that delivers a high level of quality, security and control, with precise timing for compliant, consistent blasting results.

It can be used across a variety of applications, including quarrying, mining, demolition and other specialist areas, and its high-strength detonator is suitable for initiating most priming charges. Complex firing patterns are easily transferred to the detonators using computer-based software; when implemented correctly, the highly accurate initiation timings benefit vibration control, fragmentation and overall blast optimisation.

Because initiation uses a digital firing system, every firing circuit and individual detonator is tested and recorded in the software before firing. The circuit can only be initiated by specialist firing equipment, offering greater safety and security than traditional systems.

Each electronic detonator contains a capacitor, a logic-and-timing circuit and explosives within a copper shell, and is equipped with its own connector.
