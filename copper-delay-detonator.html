Copper Delay Detonator.

SBL ENERGYNEO CDD
OVERVIEW

Reliable delay initiation for underground coal mines.

These detonators use copper shells filled with a PETN base charge combined with a primary charge. Each shell is crimped to a pair of PVC-coated wires and fitted with an electric fuse head.

They are available as the NEO CDD (PESO 634), a Permitted Copper Delay Detonator, and the NEO CED (PESO 633), a Permitted Copper Instantaneous Detonator — both PESO-approved for use in coal mines.

KEY ADVANTAGES

Why our Copper Delay Detonators stand out.

  • ✓Reliable means of delay initiation in underground coal mines
  • ✓Provides required free face for successive rows of holes in solid blasting
  • ✓Copper shells with PETN base charge — consistent performance
  • ✓PVC-coated leg wires — moisture resistant
  • ✓Both permitted delay (NEO CDD) and permitted instantaneous (NEO CED) variants
  • ✓Multiple package sizes: DBD, DBF, DBI, DBL
